## Project Overview

The **Accounting Invoice Management System** is a web application designed to streamline invoice creation, product management, and customer tracking. The platform is built to help businesses handle billing operations efficiently, reduce errors, and gain insights into revenue and client interactions.

**Objective:** Automate and simplify invoicing workflows.  
**Goal:** Improve operational efficiency for businesses of all sizes.  

---

## Key Features

<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d404b02c-de34-48c2-a069-3e883de2298c" alt="Invoice Management Dashboard" width="100%">


- **Invoice Generation:** Create, edit, and manage invoices with detailed itemization.  
- **Product Management:** Maintain an inventory of products with pricing details.  
- **Customer Management:** Centralized database of clients for seamless invoicing.  
- **User Management:** Role-based access control for admins and staff.  
- **Dashboard & Analytics:** Visualize revenue trends, outstanding invoices, and performance metrics.  
- **Export & Print Options:** Generate PDFs and print invoices directly from the system.  
- **Responsive Design:** Accessible across devices, including desktops, tablets, and mobiles.  



---

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap 5  
- **Backend:** Python, Flask, SQLAlchemy ORM  
- **Database:** SQLite/MySQL  
- **Templating & Libraries:** Jinja2, PDF generation libraries  

---

## System Architecture

- Built using the **MVC (Model-View-Controller) pattern** for clean separation of concerns.  
- **Database-driven**, storing customers, products, invoices, and user data securely.  
- **Session management** ensures secure login and role-based permissions.  


---

## Visualizations & Insights

- Dashboards and charts provide **insights into invoice trends, revenue streams, and product performance**.  
- Visual indicators highlight **overdue invoices, top-selling products, and customer activity**.  
- Helps businesses make **data-driven decisions** to optimize operations.
